Overview Of Web3

What is Web3 ?

- web3 is a lastest version of the internet that leverages blockchain technology to create a decentralized
and user-controlled digital experience.
-Web3 aims to give users ownership over their data and digital assets through decentralized networks.

How Web3 work?

1- Blockchain Infrastructure
2- Consensus Mechanisms
3- Decentralized Applications
4- Digital Wallets
5- Smart Contracts Execution

Examples of Web3 Applications:

1-Cryptocurrencies
2- Decentralized Finance
3- Non-Fungible Tokens
4- Decentralized Autonomous Organizations
5- Decentralized Storage
6- Blockchain-Based Identity Systems

Benefits of Web3:

1- Increased security and privacy
2- Transparent transactions
3- Reduced reliance on intermediaries
4- Empowered user ownership and control
